At a Glance

Key Insights from the Tracer Study

Aggregated from the official BPED BLEPT tracer survey of all 53 first-batch takers (Class of 2025).

🏆

Perfect first-take record. All 53 graduates passed the LEPT on their first attempt — a 100% institutional passing rate for the pioneer batch.

🎖️

9 national top notchers landed in the Top 5 to Top 10 nationwide, led by a Top 5 finish with a 94.20% overall rating.

🥇

49% graduated with Latin honors — 24 Cum Laude and 2 Magna Cum Laude — showing strong academic foundations before the board exam.

💪

Major subjects were the strength. The batch averaged 94.58% in the PE Major area — the highest among the three exam components.

📚

Professional Education was the challenge. 87% of takers identified Prof. Ed. as the most difficult area, matching its lowest mean rating of 87.31%.

⏱️

Preparation pays off. 72% prepared for 4–6 months, and 60% studied at least 3–5 times a week up to daily.

About the Program

Bachelor of Physical Education at MSU–GenSan

🏛️

The Program. The Bachelor of Physical Education (BPED) is offered by the College of Education of Mindanao State University – General Santos City, preparing future PE teachers in pedagogy, sports science, and movement education.

📝

The LEPT. The Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers is administered by the Professional Regulation Commission. Passers earn the Licensed Professional Teacher (LPT) title. The March 2026 exam was the first taken by BPED's pioneering Batch Maragsa.

🌟

The Legacy. A 100% first-take passing rate and 9 national top notchers set the standard for future batches — a milestone in the university's mission of fostering excellence.
